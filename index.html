Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Introduction

Orbnow (“the App”) is a service for briefly sharing the “now” of places and the world.
We respect user privacy and handle information only to the extent necessary to provide and improve the service.

Information We Collect or Handle

The App may collect or handle the following information:

  • Videos posted by users
  • Text attached to posts
  • Location-related information associated with posts
  • Technical information necessary for app operation, improvement, bug investigation, and safety

Location Information

The App uses location information to record post locations, display posts, and show nearby content.
If location blur is enabled, posted locations are displayed with an approximate blur radius of 1 km.

Purpose of Use

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Displaying posts
  • Showing posts on the map and globe
  • Providing and improving app features
  • Investigating bugs
  • Maintaining safety and security

Retention Period

Posted videos, text, and location-related post data are generally deleted automatically one hour after posting.
In some cases, deletion may be delayed briefly due to technical reasons.

Sharing with Third Parties

Except where required by law, we do not sell or improperly provide collected information to third parties.

Security

We take reasonable measures to help prevent unauthorized access, loss, or alteration of information.

Use by Minors

Minors should use the App with the consent of a parent or guardian where appropriate.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary.
If important changes are made, we will provide clear notice within the App.
